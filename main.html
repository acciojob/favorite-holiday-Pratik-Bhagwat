Maldives

The Maldives is an island nation located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of India and Sri Lanka. It is made up of 26 atolls and more than 1,000 coral islands. The Maldives is known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and vibrant marine life, making it a popular destination for honeymooners and scuba divers. The capital city is Male, which is located on the island of the same name. The official language is Dhivehi and the currency is the Maldivian rufiyaa. The Maldives is a popular destination for luxury resorts and tourism is the main contributor to the country's economy.
